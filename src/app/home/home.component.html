Tshwane Varsity Hackathon 2022

Tshwane University Of Technology's(TUT) annual Hackathon is meant to challenge information and
Communication Technology(ICT), students accross learning institutions in Tshwane Metro to demonstrate
their skills in software developement, cloud computing and agile practices while competing to address industry
and community problems through technology.
